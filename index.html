Recipes of the week

Our Recipe of the Week brings you a delicious new dish to inspire your cooking and brighten your table. Each week, we feature a fresh, flavorful recipe that’s easy to make, uses simple ingredients, and delivers big on taste. Whether you’re craving comfort food, a quick weekday meal, or something special for the weekend, our weekly recipe is designed to spark creativity in your kitchen and make every bite memorable.

Chicken & Grain Bowl

Blistered blueberries amplify the deliciousness in this beautiful warm chicken salad.

Packed with manganese for cell protection and bone health, and a good source of fibre and anthocyanins, blueberries are a delicious way to support your overall well-being.

Beef Rendang

Rendang is a wonderfully perfumed, thick, spiced stew from Indonesia and is often touted as one of the tastiest foods in the world. It normally takes hours of stirring and vigilant care, but of course using leftover brisket means it can be made in a reasonably short time, with equally tender, beautiful results.

Chicken burgers

Who doesn’t love a good chicken burger? These are perfect for summer eating, whether you’re firing up the barbecue or cooking indoors. Juicy, marinated chicken is piled into a bun with spiced yoghurt, chargrilled peppers and crisp lettuce, then served with grilled corn on the cob for an extra hit of seasonal veg. Get stuck in!
